What is a crossword?

a crossword constructor is called a cruciverbalist.

First let’s drop the word “meta.” A crossword is a puzzle that involves filling a grid with letters which will form words and phrases when read across or down. These across and down entries are filled by solving crossword clues.

Things you will need
