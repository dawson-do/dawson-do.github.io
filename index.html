Dawson’s puzzles (selected)

During undergraduate, I wrote puzzles for University of Maryland’s Puzzle Club. I became the project lead starting Fall 2019. Nowadays, I’m working on other unaffiliated events.

If you are attempting any, feel free to ask me for hints at dawsondo@umd.edu. I can also provide any number of feeder answers to any of the metapuzzles.
